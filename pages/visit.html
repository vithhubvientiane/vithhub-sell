How to Get There

🚌 From Thailand (Nong Khai)

Cross the Friendship Bridge at Nong Khai → Free shuttle provided from Vientiane side → 15 min to Big C. Passport required.

✈️ By Air

Wattay International Airport, Vientiane → Taxi or tuk-tuk to Big C. 20–30 minutes, ~80,000–120,000 LAK.

🚗 By Car / Self-Drive

Big C Vientiane has ample free parking. From city center: 5 minutes via Route 13 South.

🛺 Tuk-Tuk / Grab

Available from Vientiane city center and major hotels. Ask for "Big C Vientiane" — most drivers know it.
